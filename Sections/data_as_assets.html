Explain the value of data and information.

Why invest in Security?

In short, cybersecurity is a crucial aspect of the 21st century and will continue to be essential for businesses. You wouldn’t want your bank information or medical records exposed to the world, right? If that concerns you, it should, because companies must uphold their responsibility to protect your data. Businesses should safeguard their assets—including hardware, software, and data—just as they protect their customers. With the rise of cyberattacks and the evolving tactics of cybercriminals, data and information assets are increasingly vulnerable to theft. The information at risk is often highly valuable to those who have no legitimate reason to access it.
